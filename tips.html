Tips when applying for a job

  1. When applying for one of our jobs, please read the contract carefully so there is no ,,hidden fees,, on your side. Our employeers are all certified business people that are looking for hard working people to work for them as a team. You can see the contracts here.
  2. When you apply for a job, make sure you send your full CV and work experience to employeer. If you have an online portfolio of some sort, you can also send that as a benefit. Our studies show that over 90% of our users land their first job by sending portfolios !
  3. Always be direct with your employeer. Clear things like work time, salary, vacations and all the other stuff first ! They are also bound to contract with us, so both sides are protected by our policy !
  4. If a job that you apply on is a part-time job you can contact us and we will help you find another part-time or even full time job if you want to work 2 jobs !
  5. Dont forget to rate the employeers so rest of the community can also engage in business with them ! If you are satisfied with the job that you found, contact us and tell us about it !
